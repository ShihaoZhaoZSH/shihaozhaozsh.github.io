About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Hong Kong supervised by Prof. Kwan-Yee K. Wong. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees at the School of Computer Science at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Yu-Gang Jiang, Prof. Xingjun Ma and Prof. Zuxuan Wu. My research interests are centered on generative AI and robust learning. I previously worked as a research intern at Microsoft, advised by Dr. DongDong Chen and Dr. Jianmin Bao. Now, I am the co-founder of Morph Studio, which specializes in video generation technology.
